# Moony's World — Baby Registry

A single-page baby gift registry for Marina & Ryan (Melbourne) to share with family in Brazil, Italy & Adelaide.

## What it is
- Cream, playful "Moony's World" nursery aesthetic (Baloo 2 display font, Nunito body).
- Gift items grouped in colour-coded sections; each links to a product and stores.
- "I've got this!" claiming so no one buys duplicates.
- PT / IT / EN language toggle.
- "Sending a gift?" panel with shipping address (tap to reveal).
